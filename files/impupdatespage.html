Registration Form fill up of 2020 Batch & Lateral Entry 2019 Batch

Written by Super User

REF: AKP/ED/GEN/21/13

Date: 25.03.2021

NOTICE

2020 Batch & Lateral Entry 2019 Batch

Students of 2020 Batch & Lateral Entry 2019 Batch who could not fill up their Registration Form can fill up their Registration from with a late fine (Rs. 550/-) from 26.03.2021 to 31.03.21

Forms have to be filled online at www.sbtejharkhand.nic.in linked to GYANJOYTI. Late Registration Fee of Rs. 550/- should be deposited in Punjab National Bank through Jharkhand University challan which can be downloaded from the above website. Students are required to personally submit the print out of their Registration form along with Bank Challan (Board Copy), photo copy of seat allotment letter and matric marks sheet to the Examination Deptt. for verification on the same day or next very day.

Note : Students who have been provisionally admitted have to submit their SLC / CLC and Residential certificate to the college before 31.03.2021

(1) All the students have to clear their dues. If the dues are not cleared then the form of the student will not be submitted by the Examination Deptt. to J. U. T portal.

(2) Students will be completely responsible for any error while filling up the form. So fill up the Registration Form sincerely.

(3) Students will not be entertained after the last date. i.e., 31.03.2021

(4) Keep the following documents ready before filling registration form.

(i) Scanned photograph in jpg / jpeg format: The size of the scanned photograph must not be more than 500KB. Photograph should be same as given to the college.

(ii) Scanned signature in jpg / jpeg format: The applicant has to sign on white paper with Black / Blue pen and the size of the scanned signature should not more than 500KB.
(iii) Scanned left thumb impression in jpg / jpeg format: The size of the left thumb scanned image must not be more than 500KB.
(iv) Mobile no. and e-Mail ID: Mobile no. and e-Mail ID should be verified for further process.

Controller of Examination
